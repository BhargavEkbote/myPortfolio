About Me

I am a student who likes to work on hobby projects. I usually work on small web-based projects individually that do only one thing but do it well.

Projects

LetsChat

The current not big but not small (code lines wise) project I maintain is a project LetsChat. Its intent is to be a base on top of which a Social Media platform can be built. It does the basic stuff that defines a social media platform and also includes some features that not all but most social media platforms contain.

I otherwise work on smaller projects that are web-based, most of them I create in 4 days and after a few patches I never update them again, LetsChat being the exception. It is still a hobby project, but I plan to keep it around long-term. A lot of my smaller projects get merged into LetsChat as new features.

NeoChat

NeoChat is a unique one, its not my main project yet its not one of the ones I abandon after some time.

Its a proof-of-concept for how to build a website using LetsChat.
